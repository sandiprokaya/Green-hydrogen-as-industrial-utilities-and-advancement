--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
This model represents a harness for calibration of fuel cell (FC) parameters.
Data for I-V and Power characteristic curves is extracted from a datasheet by an industrial FC supplier.  
Specific properties such as min/max H2 and air flows are also taken from the same source.
